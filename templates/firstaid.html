🩹 First Aid Guide

❤️ Stay Safe

🤕 Head Injury

• Stay calm
• Apply a cold pack
• Watch for dizziness or vomiting
• Visit a doctor if symptoms worsen

🔥 Burns

• Cool under running water for 20 minutes
• Do not apply toothpaste or ice
• Cover with a clean cloth

🩸 Bleeding

• Apply direct pressure
• Raise injured area if possible
• Use a clean bandage

🐍 Snake Bite

• Keep patient calm
• Don't cut the wound
• Don't suck the venom
• Go to hospital immediately

❤️ CPR

• Call emergency services
• Push hard and fast in the center of chest
• About 100–120 compressions per minute

🦴 Fracture

• Don't move injured part
• Use a splint if possible
• Seek medical care immediately

⚠ Emergency Notice

LifeLine AI provides first-aid guidance only. Always contact emergency medical services for serious injuries.
